# **TableTurnerr: Business Overview**

## **1\. What is TableTurnerr?**

At its core, **TableTurnerr** is a specialized **SEO website development and traffic generation agency** built exclusively for independent restaurants.

The restaurant industry is currently skewed: large chains have the capital to build powerful digital infrastructures, while independent restaurants stay stuck because they lack the digital presence to "qualify" for enterprise-level tools. TableTurnerr bridges this gap. The company acts as an outsourced digital team, taking raw, independent restaurants and driving massive organic and paid traffic to them so they can scale their brand and compete with major chains.

## **2\. Core Services**

TableTurnerr offers a comprehensive suite of services to get restaurants "scale-ready" before handing them the keys to advanced tech platforms. Clients can utilize any of these services based on their specific needs:

* **Custom SEO Websites & Traffic Generation:** As a traffic-first company, TableTurnerr builds high-performing, conversion-optimized websites tailored specifically for the food and beverage industry. The agency drives customers to these sites through two primary channels: **aggressive organic SEO and paid Google Ads**. This dual traffic strategy helps restaurants build the minimum web volume required to eventually qualify for elite platforms like Owner.com. *(Note: TableTurnerr is not a POS system and does not handle payment processing. They simply generate traffic and redirect those online sales to the restaurant's existing POS ordering system—unless the client transitions to Owner.com or ChowNow).*  
* **Brand & Design Work:** A complete aesthetic refresh, including modern menu design, promotional poster design, and overall brand identity enhancement.  
* **Digital Storefront Optimization:** Setup and optimization of Google Business Profiles, local SEO, and customer review management to ensure high visibility.  
* **Qualification & Tech Prep:** Auditing the restaurant's operations to ensure they meet the strict quality standards required by enterprise tech platforms.  
* **Ecosystem Onboarding:** Serving as the bridge to advanced, commission-free ordering solutions. TableTurnerr partners with ChowNow to provide clients with a robust **order-taking system**, and highly recommends Owner.com for clients who qualify and are seeking a **complete, all-in-one system**.

## **3\. The Recommended Growth Path**

While clients can pick and choose services, TableTurnerr highly recommends a proven, chronological roadmap to maximize a restaurant's digital growth and revenue:

1. **Step 1: The Foundation.** TableTurnerr builds a highly-converting, SEO-optimized website and handles necessary branding to instantly scale the client's online presence and satisfy the strict qualification requirements (like minimum web traffic) of elite software partners.  
2. **Step 2: The Transition.** Once qualified, the client is seamlessly onboarded to a world-class, commission-free direct ordering system. TableTurnerr highly recommends and primarily suggests **Owner.com** as the complete system and current undisputed "king" of the restaurant tech industry that delivers significantly superior results. For smaller operations, ChowNow remains available as a budget-friendly order-taking alternative.  
3. **Step 3: The Scale.** With the ultimate foundation and ordering system in place, TableTurnerr helps the client aggressively invest in revenue-driving marketing channels. This includes scaling Google Ads (paid traffic), deploying ongoing branding strategies, and implementing smart systems to consistently capture 5-star Google reviews and drive repeat direct orders.

## **4\. Business & Revenue Model**

TableTurnerr operates a dual-revenue business model, combining traditional agency service fees with strategic tech partnerships:

* **1\. Agency Service Fees (The Build Phase):** TableTurnerr does not use rigid, fixed pricing. Because every restaurant's needs, size, and existing branding differ, **the price of services varies completely per project**. The agency provides custom quotes for the upfront heavy lifting: building the website, designing the menus, and optimizing the client's Google presence.  
* **2\. Strategic Partnership Perks (The Transition Phase):** As an **Official Strategic Partner of platforms like Owner.com and ChowNow**, TableTurnerr acts as a highly qualified lead generator for these tech ecosystems. For Owner.com, TableTurnerr secures exclusive financial benefits for the client (waiving the $970 setup fee). For ChowNow, they provide a streamlined setup for a budget-friendly alternative, including **1 month free** if the client signs up for an annual plan. In return, TableTurnerr earns partnership commissions and referral rewards from the tech platforms for successful client onboarding.

## **5\. Target Audience**

* **The Ideal Client:** Independent restaurants with physical locations that want to increase direct online orders, value great design and customer experience, and are looking for a long-term growth partner.  
* **Disqualifiers (Who TableTurnerr avoids):** Major corporate chains, ghost kitchens with no physical storefront, restaurants looking for cheap, one-off projects, or owners who refuse to adapt their digital strategy. *(Note: TableTurnerr does not expect restaurants to leave third-party apps entirely; rather, they disqualify those who refuse to transition those apps into a marketing tool instead of their primary lifeline).*

## **6\. The Strategic Tech Advantage (Owner.com & ChowNow)**

While TableTurnerr builds the foundation and the brand, the agency leverages industry-leading platforms like **Owner.com** and **ChowNow** as their ultimate tech stack recommendations. Depending on the exact budget and needs of the restaurant, TableTurnerr provides a massive competitive advantage:

* **The Owner.com Route (Premium Complete System):** For restaurants ready to scale aggressively. TableTurnerr waives the standard **$970 onboarding fee** and transitions the client to an all-in-one platform that offers commission-free online ordering, automated marketing, a custom mobile app, and AI-powered upselling & loyalty programs for a predictable $500/month flat fee (paid to Owner.com).  
* **The ChowNow Route (Budget-Friendly Order System):** For cost-conscious clients who still want a powerful digital ecosystem. TableTurnerr suggests ChowNow (which typically runs at **$199/month**) to give them commission-free deliveries, a dedicated website, a mobile app, direct ordering capabilities, and Google & social media integrations. **Bonus:** If TableTurnerr's clients sign up for a ChowNow annual plan, they get **1 month completely free**.  
* **The 3rd-Party Pivot Strategy:** Regardless of which platform a client chooses, TableTurnerr's goal is *not* to force them off third-party delivery apps (UberEats, DoorDash). Instead, the agency helps them flip the script: clients will use those high-commission platforms strictly as a **marketing channel to acquire new customers**, while actively converting those users to their new direct, commission-free platform for all repeat business.  
* **Ongoing Support & Long-Term Advocacy:** Once a client transitions to Owner.com or ChowNow, the respective platform handles the day-to-day management of the website, app, and ordering systems. However, TableTurnerr's relationship doesn't end at handoff. The agency offers supplementary ongoing services—such as scaling Google Ads and continuous design work—to fill the marketing gaps that the tech platforms don't cover. Beyond that, TableTurnerr cares deeply about client satisfaction and success. If a client ever experiences issues with these tech partners, TableTurnerr steps in to advocate for the restaurant and get the issues resolved. If a platform ultimately isn't the right fit, TableTurnerr provides objective, business-first advice—whether that means switching the client to a better-suited partner, having TableTurnerr's own team take over specific needs, or advising the client on an entirely different path. TableTurnerr puts the restaurant's success above all else.
